My Favorite Hobby

One of my favorite hobbies is playing basketball. I enjoy the energy of the game, working on my shooting, dribbling, and teamwork. Basketball keeps me active, motivated, and always improving. I also love watching the NBA, especially my favorite team, the Miami Heat.
